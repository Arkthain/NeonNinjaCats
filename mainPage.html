About Neon Ninja Cats!

We are a European esports team looking to become a premire brand within the industry! We have excellent teams for League of legends, DOTA 2, CSGO and Rainbow 6 Siege! We are looking to expand into other popular games. As an org we produce quality behind the scenes content and documentaries so that our fans can follow their favourite players through their gaming careers!

With the regular season for league of legends over and our unfortunate failure to attend worlds this year we take a look back at the highlights from our 2020 season!
